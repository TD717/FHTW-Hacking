Forensics

What is Forensics ?

Forensics is the practice and study of investigating digital evidence to uncover hidden information in the context of cybersecurity. In simpler terms, it involves analyzing files, memory dumps, logs, or network traffic to find clues or data (often called "flags") that solve challenges. These tasks often require digging into low-level system details and understanding how data is stored, transmitted, or concealed.
