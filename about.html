About Us

FHTW Hacking is a cybersecurity learning platform developed by students of the University of Applied Sciences of Vienna (Fachhochschule Technikum Wien) as part of an academic project.

Our goal is to make cybersecurity knowledge accessible, engaging, and practical for everyone, regardless of their prior experience. We believe that true learning happens through hands-on practice, which is why our platform offers beginner-friendly guides, interactive challenges, and a live scoreboard to track your achievements.

Built with a passion for technology and security, FHTW Hacking combines academic insights with real-world challenges, allowing users to develop essential skills in a supportive environment. Whether you are starting your first steps or aiming to refine your expertise, our platform is the perfect launchpad for your cybersecurity journey. Join us today and unlock your potential in the world of cybersecurity!
